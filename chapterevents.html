Chapter Events

Each semester, the Alpha Lambda chapter hosts chapter and executive board meetings, puts on professional events, and participates in service hours for the community. We participate in events in school events at Washington University, including Dance Marathon, Relay for Life and Thurtene Carnival. As well, we spend time together as a brotherhood building our lifelong bonds and friendship. Our calendar of events is updated regularly, so check back to see what else happening with AKPsi.
