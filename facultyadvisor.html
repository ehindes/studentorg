Yoon Groves joined the Olin staff in 2012 in her current role as the an Associate Director of Academic and Student Services, as well as a student advisor. She additionally works closely with student groups in the business school, including Alpha Kappa Psi, through the Olin Executive Council. Yoon received a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration at the University of Missouri-Columbia and a Master of Arts in Student Personnel Administration from Saint Louis University.
