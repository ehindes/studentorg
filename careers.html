AKPsi Careers
Join the World's Largest Professional Network

Alpha Kappa Psi's extensive professional network provides a wide range of connections and advantages in the job market. As the world's largest business fraternity, our brothers have a variety of experiences and can gain valuable leadership and interpersonal skills working with the chapters. Whether you are interested in working in fields ranging from biomedical engineering to design to finance, Alpha Kappa Psi can help you develop the professional skills necessary to navigate your career.

Our Professional Events

Each semester, the Alpha Lambda chapter hosts a variety of professional events. We are always looking to co-program with other student organizations or working with employers. Please contact our VP of Professional at wu.akpsi@gmail.com for any questions or inquiries regarding our professional events.
