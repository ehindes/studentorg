The Premier Developer of Principled Business Leaders

Alpha Kappa Psi is a professional business fraternity at Washington University in St. Louis dedicated to building brotherhood, lifelong learning, high ethical standards, improving communities, and enhancing the fraternity for life. The Alpha Lambda chapter is commited to the development of the fraternity through brotherhood bonding, assisting in achieving professional and career goals, and giving back to the community through service events. We are proud to be a business fraternity promoting professional opportunities for all majors on campus.

Announcements
Tuesday, April 14, 2015

Our Spring 2015 Pledge Class was initiated into the brotherhood! The Alpha Lambda chapter is proud of all that you have accomplished this semester during the pledging process and are looking forward to all of your professional development, personal growth and overall success as brothers. We cannot wait to see what you will bring to the fraternity.

Thursday, April 23, 2015

The Alpha Lambda chapter hosted its end-of-semester Yellow Rose Formal at Gallery 400 in Downtown St. Louis in celebration of our brotherhood and all of our hard work these past few months. As well, we had the opportunity to say goodbye to the graduating seniors, as this was one of their last events and their last formal with the chapter.

Sunday, April 26, 2015

At Elections, the Alpha Lambda chapter voted to fill all semester-long positions for Fall 2015 and the three full year positions President, Treasurer and VP Human Resources for the 2015-2016 academic year. Congratulations to our new Executive Board! We cannot wait to see what new ideas you will bring to your leadership roles.
